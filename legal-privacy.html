LegalLegacy Letter™

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
⚠ This is a working template prepared for review by legal counsel. It is not yet final and should not be relied upon until reviewed and approved.

1. What we collect

  • Account data — name, email, and (optionally) phone, via our sign-in provider (Clerk).
  • Your Messages — text you write, audio/video you record, transcripts, and the delivery rules you set. This is deeply personal content; we treat it that way.
  • People you add — names and contact details of Recipients and Trusted Contacts you provide. You are responsible for having the right to share them.
  • Billing data — handled by Stripe/PayPal; we never see or store full card numbers.
  • Operational data — check-ins, verification events, delivery records, and an audit log of actions on your account.

2. How we use it

Only to operate the Service: storing and delivering your Messages as you direct, transcribing recordings (OpenAI Whisper), powering optional AI writing suggestions (Anthropic Claude — your draft text is sent to the model only when you tap an AI button), sending the emails/SMS the product requires (invitations, verification, proof-of-life, delivery notices), processing payments, and keeping the Service secure. We do not sell personal data and we do not use your Messages for advertising or to train AI models.

3. Who can see your Messages

Before delivery: only you. Trusted Contacts cannot read your Messages while you are alive; they see only their role and status. After verified death, Messages are released only to their intended Recipients per your rules. Our staff access content only where strictly necessary to operate the Service or as required by law.

4. Sharing with our family of products

Legacy Letter™ is operated alongside iFinallyWill™ and Pet Protection Promise™. If you are an iFinallyWill™ customer, we confirm your entitlement with iFinallyWill™ using your email address so your benefits apply here. We share only what entitlement requires — never the content of your Messages.

5. Processors we rely on

Clerk (authentication & encrypted account data), Vercel (hosting), Resend (email), Twilio (SMS), Cloudflare R2/AWS S3 (media storage), Stripe & PayPal (payments), Anthropic (AI suggestions), OpenAI (transcription). Each receives only the minimum needed for its function.

6. Retention & death

We keep your content while your account is active so it can be delivered — that is the point of the Service, and it may mean retaining content for many years, including after your death until delivery completes. You can delete Messages or your account at any time before delivery; deletion is permanent.

7. Security

Content is encrypted in transit and at rest by our providers; access is token-authenticated; delivery links are time-limited; sensitive operations are signed and logged. No system is perfectly secure, and we will notify you of any breach as the law requires.

8. Your rights

You can access, correct, export (Estate Dashboard → "Export my data"), or delete your data. Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, PIPEDA, CCPA) you may have further rights; contact us to exercise them.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16. Recipients may be minors; their messages are held until the delivery conditions you set are met.
